IMS StepUp SA is a South African footwear retailer seeking to improve marketing performance, sales visibility, customer retention, and digital channel effectiveness.

This project delivers a complete analytics pipeline, transforming raw operational data into executive dashboards that support strategic business decisions across marketing, retail operations, customer analytics, and website performance.

Project Scope

  • Marketing campaigns
  • Store transactions
